Description

Here is a delicious lasagna recipe that can be made ahead of time and kept in the freezer uncooked for when you need it during the week!

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp Olive Oil
  • 750g Lean Beef Mince
  • 90g Prosciutto
  • 800g Passata
  • 200ml Hot Beef Stock
  • Nutmeg
  • 300g Fresh Lasagne Sheets
  • White Sauce
  • 125 Mozzarella

Steps

  1. To make the meat sauce, heat the olice oil in a frying pan, and cook 750g of beef mince in two batches for about 10 minutes
  2. Finely chop 4 slices of prosciutto, then stir through the meat mixture
  3. Pour over 800g passata and 200ml hot beef stock, add a little grated nutmeg and season
  4. Bring up to the boil and then simmer for 30 mins
  5. Heat the oven to 180c/160c fan/gas 4 and lightly oil an ovenproof dish
  6. Spoon one third of the meat sauce into the dish, then cover with some fresh lasagne sheets from a 300g pack. Drizzle over roughly 130g ready-made white sauce
  7. Repeat until you have three layers of pasta. Cover with the remaining 390g white sauce, making sure you can't see any pasta poking through
  8. Scatter 125g torn mozzarella over the top
  9. Arrange the rest of the prosciutto on top. Bake for 45 minutes until the top is bubbling and lightly browned
